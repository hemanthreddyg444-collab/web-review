Understanding Food Waste in the World

Food waste occurs at every stage from farm to fork and contributes a significant share of global greenhouse gas emissions.

Global Food Waste Snapshot

1.05B tons of food wasted annually worldwide.

60% of waste comes from households.

Recovering just 25% could significantly reduce hunger.

Food Waste by Sector

Households: Overbuying and confusion about dates.
Food Service: Restaurants, hotels, canteens.
Retail: Strict cosmetic standards.
Farms & Processing: Harvest and transport losses.

Most Wasted Food Types

Fruits & Vegetables: High spoilage.
Bread & Cereals: Frequent household waste.
Dairy & Eggs: Sensitive to storage and dates.
Prepared Meals: Leftovers and buffets.
